About Us

Our goal is to make climate change research more accessible by providing open source hardware and software.

6+ years in business
3 open source products launched
4 locations with employees

Creating technology to measure the impact of climate change

We develop hardware and software to assess the impact of climate change. We help organizations and researchers with cost-effective sensor solutions for collecting environmental and plant physiology information in addition to providing platform to run workflows.

Dotmote Labs is a startup located within Startup Hall at the University of Washington, working in partnership with CoMotion Labs.
