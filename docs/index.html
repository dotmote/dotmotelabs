Our customers

Our products at work

The Nature Conservancy

The assessment of microclimates within forests is a critical tool for gaining future insights into how climate change impacts can be mitigated. The Nature Conservancy (TNC) and Dotmote Labs have joined forces in a partnership focused on comprehending climate dynamics in secondary forests.

The United States Department of Agriculture

The USDA is keen on investigating sap flow dynamics by employing Dotmote Labs' Sapflow sensor. This research aims to comprehend the variations in stress response among corn plants.
