About Us

Dotmote Labs is building the next generation of climate-intelligence infrastructure. Born out of academia and engineered for scale, we provide cloud-enabled AI workflow technology and intelligent sensing systems that empower researchers, organizations, and industries to understand environmental change in real time.

Our Mission

To accelerate climate and environmental research through scalable, cloud-based AI workflows and accessible sensing technology — enabling faster, smarter, data-driven decisions.

What We Do

Cloud-Enabled AI Workflow Processing

Our platform turns complex scientific and environmental analysis into automated, scalable workflows. From remote sensing to plant-physiology analytics, we deliver serverless, cloud-native tools that cut time-to-insight dramatically.

High-Impact Environmental Sensing

We design open, modular, and affordable hardware — from sap-flow and canopy sensors to micro-climate systems — giving institutions and enterprises high-resolution data without heavy infrastructure overhead.

Integrated Intelligence Platforms

We combine IoT data, cloud pipelines, and AI models into intuitive platforms that scale from small research teams to nationwide deployments.

Why It Matters

Climate and environmental intelligence is becoming a critical infrastructure category. As climate risks rise, organizations need reliable, high-frequency, actionable data — not months-delayed insights.

Dotmote Labs sits at the intersection of AI, cloud automation, and climate technology, uniquely positioned to serve academia, agriculture, forestry, conservation, and emerging climate-risk markets.

Our Edge

Born out of academic research, engineered for commercial scalability

Cloud-native, serverless workflow engine reduces operational cost and complexity

Hardware + software ecosystem creates defensible integration and long-term value

Rapidly growing demand for climate-intelligence tools across industries

Vision

We're building the infrastructure layer for climate research and monitoring — a unified platform where environmental data is captured, processed, analyzed, and deployed at global scale. Our goal is to empower researchers and organizations everywhere to accelerate climate insights and act faster.
