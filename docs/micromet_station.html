Micro-Met Station

Our plug-and-play micro-metrological (micromet) stations are cost effective, open-source, and can be manufactured rapidly. In addition, the stand-alone gauges and micro-loggers can be easily installed outdoors with solar-panels or wired in a glasshouse.

Our advancements in providing easy to use system offers promising future in getting finer resolution environmental data closer to the ground
